Terms of Use

Guidelines for using StatStox fairly and safely.

Eligibility

Users must meet age and regional eligibility requirements.

  • Age verification required
  • Geofencing for compliance
  • One account per user

Fair Play

Projection locks, entry caps, and payout caps keep the ecosystem balanced.

  • No late entry after lock
  • No automated entry abuse
  • Respect payout and entry limits

Market Rules

StatStox is not a real stock exchange or broker.

  • Player performance only
  • No real equity ownership
  • Market fees and spreads apply

StatCoin Credits

StatCoin is an in-app credit system, not a public cryptocurrency.

  • Off-chain credits
  • Used for entries and rewards
  • Subject to payout caps

Responsible Gaming

StatStox promotes healthy play and financial safety.

  • Deposit and entry limits
  • Self-exclusion tools
  • Support resources
