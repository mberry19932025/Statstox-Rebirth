# StatStox Year 1 Financial Snapshot (Illustrative)

## Assumptions (Base Case)
- 15,000 MAU, 2,500 payers
- DFS handle per payer per month: $70
- DFS rake: 8%
- Market volume per payer per month: $120
- Market fee/spread: 1.2%
- Subscriptions: 400 users at $15/month

## Revenue (Year 1)
- DFS rake: 2,500 * $70 * 12 * 0.08 = $168,000
- Market fees: 2,500 * $120 * 12 * 0.012 = $43,200
- Subscriptions: 400 * $15 * 12 = $72,000
- Estimated total: $283,200

## Costs (Year 1)
- Payment processing (2.9% of $5.7M deposits): $165,000
- Data feeds and real-time stats: $90,000
- Cloud/infra/monitoring: $36,000
- Payroll (3-4 FTE + contractors): $420,000
- Compliance/legal: $50,000
- Marketing/community: $80,000
- Estimated total: $841,000

## Margin (Year 1)
- Estimated operating margin: -$557,800
- This is typical for early-stage DFS platforms; profitability requires scale.

## Break-Even Targets (Illustrative)
- 4x handle or 3x subscription mix
- Lower processing costs via ACH and wallet balances
- Higher market activity per payer

- All values are placeholders for investor discussions.
- Replace with real conversion and retention data once beta launches.
